TIME CRUNCH — FULL FEATURE LIST
For marketing reference
================================================================================


OVERVIEW
--------
Time Crunch is a cloud-based time tracking and field management platform built
for construction contractors and trades businesses. It covers the full workflow
from workers clocking in on a job site to admin approving hours, running payroll
reports, and billing clients — all from a phone or desktop, online or offline.

The platform has two connected apps: a Time Clock app for workers and admins,
and a Field app for job site documentation. Both are accessible from the same
login, on any device.


================================================================================
TIME TRACKING — WORKER SIDE
================================================================================

CLOCK IN / CLOCK OUT
  Workers clock in and out with a single tap. The app records the exact time,
  the project, and optionally the worker's GPS location. No paper timesheets,
  no calling in.

GPS GEOFENCING
  Admins can set a job site location and radius for any project. Workers will
  only be able to clock in if they are physically within that radius. Prevents
  buddy punching and off-site clock-ins.

BREAK AND MILEAGE TRACKING
  While clocked in, workers can log a break (in minutes) and job-related mileage
  before clocking out. Both are captured on the time entry.

CANCEL A WRONG CLOCK-IN
  If a worker accidentally clocks in to the wrong project, they can cancel the
  clock-in without creating any time entry, then clock in correctly.

MANUAL TIME ENTRY
  Workers can also log time manually — useful for entering hours after the fact.
  Fields: project, date, start time, end time, break, mileage, and notes.

EDIT AND DELETE ENTRIES
  Workers can edit or delete their own entries up to 7 days after the work date.
  Once approved by an admin, entries are locked and cannot be changed.

TIMESHEET VIEW
  A calendar-style view of a worker's own entries, grouped by week, so they can
  see their schedule at a glance.

TIMESHEET SIGN-OFF
  Workers can digitally sign off on their timesheet for a date range, indicating
  the hours are accurate. This timestamp is visible to admins in the approval
  queue.

PAY STUBS
  Workers can view their own pay stubs for each pay period — showing regular
  hours, overtime hours, prevailing wage hours, mileage, and estimated gross pay.

PDF TIMESHEET EXPORT
  Workers can export their own timesheet as a print-ready PDF from any date
  range, useful for personal records or disputes.

UPCOMING SHIFTS
  Workers see their scheduled shifts for the coming days. A single tap
  pre-fills the time entry form with the shift's project, date, and hours,
  so logging time is even faster.


================================================================================
ADMIN — APPROVAL AND OVERSIGHT
================================================================================

APPROVAL QUEUE
  All submitted time entries land in the admin's approval queue. Admins can
  approve or reject entries one at a time, or approve all entries at once.
  Rejections include an optional reason note that is visible to the worker.

FILTER BY WORKER
  When the queue has entries from multiple workers, admins can filter to a
  single worker and approve or reject just that person's entries.

APPROVE ALL (BULK)
  A single button approves every pending entry, or every pending entry for a
  specific worker, instantly.

LIVE DASHBOARD (REFRESHES EVERY MINUTE)
  Admins see real-time KPI cards: how many workers are clocked in right now,
  total company hours this week, pending approvals, and workers approaching or
  in overtime. Updates automatically without refreshing the page.

LIVE WORKER STATUS
  A live list of which workers are currently clocked in, what project they're
  on, and how long they've been on the clock.

COMMENTS ON TIME ENTRIES
  Admins and workers can exchange threaded comments on any individual time
  entry — useful for clarifying questions, corrections, or notes before
  approval.

BROADCAST ANNOUNCEMENTS
  Admins can send a push notification to every active worker at once — for job
  site updates, schedule changes, urgent notices, or anything else that needs
  to reach the whole team immediately.

UNUSUAL CLOCK-IN ALERTS
  If a worker clocks in outside of configured working hours (e.g., 2 AM), the
  admin automatically receives an email alert.

INACTIVE WORKER ALERTS
  The system sends the admin a daily push notification and email if any worker
  has not submitted a time entry in a configurable number of days (default: 3).
  Helps catch workers who may have stopped logging time without notice.

AUDIT LOG
  Every significant admin action (approval, rejection, rate change, pay period
  lock/unlock, etc.) is recorded in an audit log with the actor's name and
  timestamp.


================================================================================
SCHEDULING
================================================================================

SHIFT SCHEDULING
  Admins can create shifts and assign them to workers and projects, with a
  specific date and start/end time.

WORKER SHIFT VIEW
  Workers see their upcoming shifts on their dashboard and can tap to auto-fill
  a time entry from any shift.


================================================================================
PAYROLL AND COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

OVERTIME CALCULATION
  The system automatically calculates regular and overtime hours for each
  worker. Supports both daily overtime (e.g., over 8 hours in a day) and weekly
  overtime (e.g., over 40 hours in a week). Threshold and multiplier are
  configurable per company.

PREVAILING WAGE TRACKING
  Projects can be flagged as "prevailing wage." Hours on those projects are
  tracked separately from regular hours, with a configurable prevailing wage
  rate. Prevailing wage hours do not count toward overtime.

PAY PERIODS
  Admins can create pay periods (e.g., bi-weekly, semi-monthly). Once a pay
  period is locked, entries within it cannot be edited or deleted by workers.
  Pay periods appear in workers' pay stub views.

CERTIFIED PAYROLL REPORT
  Generates a weekly certified payroll report — required by law for contractors
  on government and public projects paying prevailing wages. Shows hours worked
  each day of the week (Mon–Sun), total hours, work classification (regular vs.
  prevailing), pay rate, and gross wages per worker. Printable and saveable
  as a PDF.

OVERTIME REPORT
  A summary report showing regular hours, overtime hours, prevailing hours,
  total hours, and estimated labor cost per worker for any date range.

PAYROLL CSV EXPORT
  Export a full payroll summary as a CSV — regular pay, overtime pay, prevailing
  pay, total cost, and mileage per worker — for import into payroll software
  (ADP, Gusto, Paychex, etc.).

TIME ENTRY CSV EXPORT
  Export a detailed CSV of every individual time entry for any date range, with
  filters for worker, project, and approval status. Opens directly in Excel or
  Google Sheets.

QUICKBOOKS ONLINE INTEGRATION
  Connect to QuickBooks Online via OAuth. Push time and payroll data directly
  from Time Crunch into QuickBooks, eliminating double-entry. Supports employee
  mapping between the two systems.

WORKER PAY STUBS
  Each worker can view a read-only pay stub for each pay period, showing their
  hour breakdown and estimated gross pay, calculated using their assigned hourly
  rate and the company's overtime settings.


================================================================================
ANALYTICS AND REPORTING
================================================================================

ANALYTICS DASHBOARD (ADMIN)
  Visual charts and summary stats:
    - Hours logged this week and this month
    - Number of active workers this week and this month
    - Pending approvals count
    - Daily bar chart — hours logged each day for the last 14 days
    - Weekly trend line — hours per week for the last 12 weeks
    - Top projects by hours logged (last 30 days)
    - Top workers by hours logged (last 30 days)

WORKER METRICS
  Per-worker breakdown showing their total hours, overtime, prevailing wage
  hours, and mileage over a date range — useful for performance review and
  labor cost analysis.

PROJECT REPORTS
  Per-project breakdown of hours logged, broken down by worker and date range.

LABOR COST ESTIMATES
  The overtime report and payroll export include estimated labor costs using
  each worker's assigned hourly rate and the company's overtime multiplier.


================================================================================
FIELD APP
================================================================================

The Field App is a separate but connected app (same login) for job site
documentation. Workers and admins switch between the Time Clock and Field
apps from any screen.

FIELD REPORTS
  Workers submit daily field reports — notes, observations, issues — tied to a
  specific date and GPS location. Admins can mark reports as reviewed.

DAILY REPORTS
  Structured daily job site reports with a title, date, description, and photos.
  Admins can view, review, and download reports as PDFs.

PHOTO CAPTURE
  Workers can take or attach photos directly from their phone within the Field
  app and attach them to daily reports.

PUNCHLIST
  Create and track punch list items for any project — open issues, deficiencies,
  or tasks that need to be resolved. Items can be marked complete.

TOOLBOX TALKS (SAFETY TALKS)
  Record toolbox talk / safety meeting details: title, content, who gave the
  talk, date, and project. Creates a digital record of safety training for
  compliance purposes.

PROJECT BILLING PDF
  Generate a formatted billing summary PDF per project, showing hours and labor
  costs for client invoicing.


================================================================================
COMPANY COMMUNICATION
================================================================================

COMPANY CHAT
  A company-wide group chat where workers and admins can communicate in real
  time. Messages are retained for a configurable number of days. Accessible
  from the worker dashboard.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
  Workers and admins can opt in to browser push notifications (works on iOS,
  Android, and desktop) to receive alerts for:
    - Approvals and rejections of their time entries
    - Comments on their entries from an admin
    - Broadcast announcements from admin
    - Inactive worker alerts (admin only)
    - Worker timesheet sign-offs (admin only)

ADMIN COMMENTS ON ENTRIES
  Admins can leave comments on individual time entries, visible to the worker.
  Workers can reply. Both sides are notified via push when a comment is added.


================================================================================
PLATFORM AND TECHNICAL
================================================================================

WORKS ON ANY DEVICE
  Fully responsive web app — works on iPhone, Android, tablet, and desktop. No
  app store download required. Workers can add it to their home screen for a
  native-app feel.

INSTALLABLE (PWA)
  Time Crunch is a Progressive Web App. Workers can install it to their phone
  home screen and launch it like a native app. An in-app prompt guides them
  through installation.

OFFLINE MODE
  Clock-ins and clock-outs are queued locally if the worker loses internet
  connection. Punches sync automatically when connectivity is restored — no
  data is lost.

BILINGUAL: ENGLISH AND SPANISH
  The worker-facing Time Clock app is fully available in English and Spanish.
  Workers can switch languages from their dashboard. All UI labels, buttons,
  and status messages are translated.

SELF-SERVICE REGISTRATION
  New companies sign up online — choose a company name, create an admin
  account, and start adding workers immediately. No sales call or setup required.

FREE TRIAL
  New companies get a free trial period (configurable, default 14 days) before
  being prompted to subscribe. All features are available during the trial.

WORKER INVITATIONS
  Admins invite workers by name and username. Workers receive an email with a
  link to set their own password. No admin handles worker passwords.

PASSWORD RESET AND EMAIL CONFIRMATION
  Standard forgot-password email flow. New admin accounts require email
  confirmation before logging in.

ROLE-BASED ACCESS
  Three roles: Worker (can clock in/out, submit entries, view own data),
  Admin (full company management), Super Admin (platform-level access for the
  operator).

FEATURE FLAGS
  Admins can enable or disable individual features per company — scheduling,
  analytics, company chat, and prevailing wage tracking — to simplify the UI
  for companies that don't use those features.

SUBSCRIPTION BILLING (STRIPE)
  Starter and Pro plans, billed via Stripe. Admins manage their subscription and
  billing details from within the app, including accessing the Stripe customer
  portal to update payment methods, download invoices, or cancel. Stripe
  webhooks keep subscription status in sync automatically.

COMPANY SETTINGS
  Admins configure:
    - Default hourly rate
    - Prevailing wage rate
    - Overtime rule (daily or weekly) and threshold (e.g., 8 hrs/day or 40 hrs/week)
    - Overtime multiplier (e.g., 1.5x)
    - Inactive worker alert threshold (days)
    - Unusual clock-in alert window (e.g., only alert outside 6 AM – 8 PM)
    - Chat message retention (days)

SECURITY
  - All data scoped to the company — no company can see another company's data
  - Rate limiting on login and sensitive auth endpoints
  - Helmet security headers on all API responses
  - JWT-based authentication with 8-hour token expiry
  - Approved time entries are locked and cannot be modified


================================================================================
SUMMARY: WHO THIS IS FOR
================================================================================

Time Crunch is designed for:

  - General contractors
  - Specialty trade contractors (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, concrete, etc.)
  - Landscaping and grounds maintenance companies
  - Any construction or field services business with hourly workers

It replaces paper timesheets, spreadsheets, and disconnected tools with a
single platform that covers time collection, approval, reporting, field
documentation, and payroll prep — built around how construction crews
actually work.
